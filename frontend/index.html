Simple Process

Go Cardless in 4 Steps

From registration to your first biometric payment takes less than 10 minutes.

1. Enroll

Visit an agent. Scan your 10 fingers to create your unique FID.

2. Link Cards

Connect your existing bank cards securely via the mobile app.

3. Assign

Map fingers to actions. E.g., Right Index for Food, Pinky for Panic.

4. Pay

Place your finger on any FingerPay POS. No phone, no card needed.
